Loading and unloading ships

“Falcon Company has extensive experience in the field of ship loading and unloading services. Our specialized team works very efficiently to ensure that goods and shipments are transported to their destination safely and efficiently.

  • We manage loading and unloading operations in an organized and well-planned manner. We use the latest technologies and heavy equipment to speed up loading and unloading operations, while maintaining the safety of goods at all stages.
  • Our team receives extensive training in safety procedures and handling of sensitive goods. We also adhere to all local regulations and laws to ensure our operations are fully compliant.
  • We pride ourselves on providing reliable and fast services to our customers. We are constantly improving our processes and developing innovative solutions to keep up with changing customer needs.

discharging different types of wood.

“Falcon has extensive experience in unloading and handling all types of wood, including hardwoods, softwoods, carved timbers and plywoods. We have a dedicated team who are well trained to carefully handle these delicate goods.

  • Our team is trained to recognize the unique benefits of each type of wood and how to handle them appropriately. We guarantee that all shipments will arrive at their destination without any damage or damage.
  • We use the most advanced unloading equipment, such as forklifts and advanced loading systems, to ensure that the lumber is safely and efficiently transported to the storage or transportation area. We also adhere to all occupational safety and health regulations when handling these materials.

discharging different types of iron

“Falcon has extensive experience in handling and unloading various types of ferrous materials, including steel, wrought iron, billet, cast iron, iron bricks and others.

  • We have a specialized team trained to use the latest equipment and technologies to unload these heavy goods safely and efficiently. We use forklifts with high lifting capacities, advanced conveyor belts, and innovative stacking and storage methods to ensure the safety of goods throughout the unloading process.
  • We strictly adhere to all occupational safety and health rules when handling ferrous materials. Our team receives extensive training on safe practices and emergency procedures to protect both workers and cargo.

discharging cement and clinker

“Falcon has long experience in handling the unloading and unloading of cement and clinker efficiently and safely. We have the experience and resources necessary to handle these sensitive and crushed materials with the utmost care.

  • We use advanced and safe unloading equipment such as conveyor belts and high-capacity forklifts to transport cement and clinker from ships to land transportation or storage areas. Our team is trained to carefully handle these materials to avoid any contamination or leakage.
  • We adhere to all applicable occupational safety and health rules when handling crushed materials such as cement and clinker. We ensure that all procedures are carried out professionally and safely to protect workers and the surrounding environment.
